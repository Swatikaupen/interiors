Everything you need to create each profile after you log in. Copy the bios as-is (they're within each platform's character limit). **Upwork is already set up** — there's a refresh note for it at the bottom.

> **Handle:** try `openstorey.design` first, then `openstoreydesign`, then `open.storey`. Use the same handle everywhere you can — consistency makes you findable.
> **Profile photo:** the Open Storey wordmark on cream (or a clean headshot of you for personal LinkedIn/Upwork).
> **Every "website" / link field:** your homepage with a UTM so you can track it — see [seo-and-tracking.md](seo-and-tracking.md). Default: `https://openstorey.design/?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=bio`

**Pin** the intro post and one strong project to the top of your grid.

## 2. Pinterest  *(your long-tail SEO engine — pins drive traffic for months)*
**Account:** create a **Business** account (or convert personal). **Claim your website** `openstorey.design` (Settings → Claim) — this unlocks analytics and puts your name on every pin.

**Display name:** `Open Storey — Interior Design`
**About (160 char max):**
```
Bay Area interior design studio by Swatika Upendran. Interiors where South Indian craft meets California calm. Tips, projects & curated finds. Free 15-min call.
```

**Create these boards (each = a keyword people search):**
`Living Rooms` · `Dining & Entertaining` · `Bedrooms & Calm` · `Bathrooms` · `Kitchens` · `Material Palettes` · `Vastu & Layout` · `South Asian Interiors` · `Curated Finds` · `Open Storey Projects`

**How to pin:** every portfolio image and every Curated Finds product becomes a pin. Each pin's **destination link** = the matching page on your site (with `?utm_source=pinterest&utm_medium=pin`). Write a keyword-rich title + 2-sentence description per pin. Aim for 5–10 pins/day (batch them).
