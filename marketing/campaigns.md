# Campaigns & Content Calendar

Every campaign below drives to **one action: book a free 15‑min call.**
Copy is ready to paste. Swap in your own photos. Use the UTM links from [seo-and-tracking.md](seo-and-tracking.md) so you can see what works.

## Brand voice (keep it consistent)
- Warm, considered, unhurried. Designer-as-storyteller, not salesperson.
- Signature idea: **two worlds** — the sensory richness of South India × the quiet precision of California.
- Words that fit: *story, intention, material, ritual, layered, devotion, calm, heritage.*
- Avoid: hype, exclamation-stacking, "DM me!!!", generic "transform your space."

## Your credibility assets (use these — they're real and earned)
- **Featured in Architectural Digest India** (Keha Casa flagship). Lead with this.
- Corporate interiors for **Cisco, Target, Intuit**; **Kohler Experience Center** (New Delhi); **Le Reve Cinemas** (Mumbai).
- 10+ years · 20+ projects · 3 countries · educated in Chennai, Barcelona, San Francisco.
- Services: Residential · Space Planning · Material Curation · **Vastu Consultation** · Custom Furniture · Art Direction · Retail Interiors.

## Campaign 2 — "Spaces with a story" (Portfolio spotlight series)
**Goal:** prove craft + credibility. **Window:** weeks 2–6, one project/week. **Channels:** IG (carousel + Reel), Pinterest (pin every image), Houzz (project upload), LinkedIn (the commercial ones).

Rotate one project per post. Template:
> **[Project name] — [one-line idea].**
> [2–3 sentences: the challenge, the material/▌spatial move, the feeling.]
> [Credibility line, e.g., "Featured in Architectural Digest India."]
> Swipe for the details that make it. Planning something similar? Free 15‑min call — link in bio.

**Project angles to use:**
- **Keha Casa** — "where form meets sensation." *Lead the whole series with this; it's the AD feature.*
- **Cisco Breakout Spaces (Bengaluru)** — "the architecture of deep focus." (Great for LinkedIn / commercial leads.)

- **Kohler Experience Center (New Delhi)** — material storytelling at retail scale.
- A **residential foyer / dining** piece — "first impressions, last forever" (best for IG + Pinterest reach).
